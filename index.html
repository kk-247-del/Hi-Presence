I. Core Architecture (Live)

Engine

Moment Persistence

Hi Presence creates ephemeral environments that dissolve the instant focus is lost. No message objects are ever created; interaction is a live, shared state.

Protocol

E2EE Live Text

Characters are encrypted and streamed in real-time. Typing is the act of communication. There is no "Send" primitive.

Web Bridge

One-Time Address

Generate a Presence Address to invite colleagues via the web. Frictionless join with a 3-Moment structural limit.

Compliance

Privacy by Constraint

Our Privacy Policy is simple: We do not store what we cannot see. Since Hi Presence rejects the "Message Object," there is no archive to breach.

  • Zero Knowledge: All text and voice are E2EE. Our backend acts as a blind switchboard.
  • Memory Only: Data exists only in volatile RAM and is wiped upon Moment collapse.
  • No Tracking: We do not collect metadata or interaction history.
