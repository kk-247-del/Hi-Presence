Security & Compliance

Privacy by Constraint

At House of Ariaka, privacy is not a feature; it is the structural ground of our system. Privacy is a structural impossibility of data persistence.

  • Zero Knowledge: All content is End-to-End Encrypted (AES-256). House of Ariaka servers never see your data.
  • Volatile Memory: Interaction data exists only in RAM and is purged instantly upon Moment collapse.
  • The Law of Collapse: Any violation of focus (screen lock/backgrounding) destroys the session and the ephemeral keys.
  • Hardware Integrity: Microphone and Camera are only accessible during active, foregrounded Moments.
