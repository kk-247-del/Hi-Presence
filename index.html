Communication Without Archiving

Hi Presence is an environment for Co-presence, rejecting the traditional messaging metaphor of data exchange. We provide the tools for immediate, high-fidelity interaction across platforms without ever creating a message object.

The Web Bridge

No-Install Seamless Access

Collaborate with anyone instantly. Generate a one-time Presence Address and share the link. Your colleagues can join the Moment through a browser without installing the app. Note: Web access is limited to 3 sessions per user to maintain architectural integrity.

Upcoming Feature

Media Reveal: Sharing Without Sending

We are breaking the file-sharing assumption. Documents, photos, and videos are never transmitted or shared as files. Instead, they are Revealed.
